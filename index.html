Planning the Webpage.

We are designing a webpage for a startup called "Nest Genomics". Nest Genomics is a software that can make a personalized medical treatment plan for you based on your genetic testing results. It recommends imaging, specialists, and medications based on your results. For example, it detects that a user is at risk for breast cancer. Nest will then come up with a personalized plan specifically for the illness, including treatment.
